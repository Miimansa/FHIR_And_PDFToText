6. PROTOCOL TREATMENT
6.1. Treatment Terminology The following terms will be used to describe and define protocol treatment:  Bridging therapy refers to treatment used to control a subject’s disease prior to conditioning chemotherapy  Debulking therapy refers to treatment used to reduce a subject’s disease prior to conditioning chemotherapy  The conditioning chemotherapy regimen used for this study will be fludarabine and cyclophosphamide.  The investigational product for this study is named axicabtagene ciloleucel.  The term study treatment refers to all protocol required therapies.
